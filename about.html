About One Support Disability

Compassionate, person-centred NDIS support in Melbourne — built on respect, dignity and genuine relationships.

Who we are

Care that starts with the person

One Support Disability was founded on a simple belief: that quality support should fit your life, not the other way around. We're a Melbourne-based, registered NDIS provider supporting participants to live with independence and dignity.

We focus on clear communication and building strong, respectful relationships — because the best support comes from people who truly understand you. From everyday tasks to long-term goals, our team is here to walk alongside you.

Get in touch

Our mission

To empower NDIS participants through quality, person-centred care — fostering independence, choice and dignity in everything we do.

Our vision

A community where every person living with disability has the support, opportunity and respect they need to live life on their own terms.

What guides us

Our values

The principles behind every interaction, every plan and every visit.

Compassion

We lead with empathy and kindness, treating everyone the way we'd want our own family treated.

Respect & dignity

Your choices, culture and identity are always honoured. You're in the driver's seat.

Independence

We build skills and confidence so you can do more of what matters, your way.

Integrity

Honest, transparent and accountable — we do what we say we'll do.

Inclusion

Everyone belongs. We celebrate diversity and support people of all backgrounds and abilities.

Reliability

Consistent, dependable support you can plan your life around.

Our approach

Person-centred, every step of the way

No two people are the same — so no two support plans should be either. We take the time to understand your goals, preferences and routine before we ever suggest a plan.

  • We listen first and tailor support to you
  • Carefully matched, qualified support workers
  • Regular check-ins to keep support working
  • Families and carers kept in the loop
