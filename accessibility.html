Accessibility statement

Everyone deserves an equal experience online. We're committed to making this website usable for people of all abilities.

Our commitment

As a disability support provider, accessibility is at the heart of what we do - and that includes our website. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA and continually work to improve the experience for everyone.

Accessibility features on this site

  • Adjustable text size - use the A− / A / A+ controls at the top of every page.
  • Full keyboard navigation with a visible focus indicator and a "skip to content" link.
  • Strong colour contrast that meets AA standards for readable text.
  • Descriptive text alternatives for images and meaningful icons.
  • Clear, semantic structure with proper headings and landmarks for screen readers.
  • Reduced-motion support - animations are minimised if your device requests it.
  • Responsive design that works across phones, tablets and computers.

Need this information another way?

If you need any information on this site in a different format - such as large print, Easy Read or over the phone - we're glad to help. Please contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

Give us feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this website. If you encounter a barrier or have a suggestion, please email info@onesupportdisability.com.au. We take all feedback seriously and aim to respond promptly.
