Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your personal information.

1. Who we are

One Support Disability ("we", "us", "our") is a registered NDIS provider based in Melbourne, Victoria. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. Information we collect

We may collect personal information including:

  • Your name, contact details and date of birth
  • NDIS number and plan information
  • Health, disability and support-related information
  • Information about your goals, preferences and circumstances
  • Information provided by family, carers or support coordinators (with consent)

3. How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide, coordinate and improve your supports
  • Communicate with you and respond to enquiries
  • Meet our obligations under the NDIS and relevant laws
  • Process referrals, employment applications and feedback

4. Disclosure of information

We only share your information where necessary - for example, with your consent, with other providers involved in your care, or where required by law. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Storing & protecting your information

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure, including physical, electronic and procedural safeguards. We retain information only for as long as required for our services and legal obligations.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you. To do so, please contact us using the details below.

7. Website & cookies

Our website may use basic cookies and analytics to improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Contact us

If you have a question or concern about your privacy, or wish to make a complaint, please contact us at info@onesupportdisability.com.au. We will respond as quickly as possible.
