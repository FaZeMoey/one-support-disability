Live independently with support

Your home, your independence

Supported Independent Living (SIL) helps you live as independently as possible in your own home. We provide the everyday support you need — building skills, confidence and routines — so you can enjoy greater freedom and control over your life.

Whether you live alone or share with others, we tailor support around your goals, with options ranging from a few scheduled hours to round-the-clock care.
